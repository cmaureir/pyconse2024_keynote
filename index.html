The Compilation Process

  • 1. Tokenizing - Text to words
  • 2. Parsing - Words to Semantics
  • 3. AST - Semantics structure
  • 4. Flow Graph - Construct the control flow graph and optimizations
  • 5. Emit bytecode - To be executed on the VM

Python Software

Foundation

The mission of the Python Software Foundation is to promote, protect, and advance the Python programming language, and to support and facilitate the growth of a diverse and international community of Python programmers.

PSF Structure

  • US 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
  • Board of directors elected by its members and the executive director (Deb Nicholson)
  • Steering Council
  • Employed Staff (12 people)

PSF Activities

  • Infrastructure (python.org, PyPI, Docs, other services)
  • Intellectual property (trademarks and licenses)
  • Community support (conferences, educational resources, funding python events)
Office hours (Board and Grants)

PSF - The Board

Governing body that oversees the foundation's mission, finances, stragic direction and governance.
  • Governance - responsible for making decisions about the foundation's policies, operations and finances.
  • Mission support - ensures the foundation fulfills its mission to support Python development and the Python community worldwide.
  • Advocacy - Advocate for the language, supporting educational efforts, community projects, and the global growth of Python usage.

PSF - The Python language

The Python developmnent is handled by the Python Core Developers Team and governed by the Python steering council.
